Rabbit

A female rabbit is called a doe and a male rabbit is called a buck.

  • Scientific Name: Leporidae
  • Average Ear Length: 10cm long
  • Eating Habits: Grass and leafy weeds
  • Habitat: meadows and forests

Rabbits are small, furry, mammals with long ears, short fluffy tails, and strong, large hind legs. They have 2 pairs of sharp incisors (front teeth), one pair on top and one pair on the bottom.
